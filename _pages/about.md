Reezon Technology was founded on the belief that technology should serve a purpose—one that’s grounded in real value, not hype. We help businesses of all sizes navigate an ever-changing technology landscape, providing clarity and direction where complexity often prevails. From strategy and digital transformation to talent development and innovation support, our goal is to help organizations make confident, informed decisions that move them forward.

Based in Howard County, Maryland, Reezon Technology takes pride in working closely with local businesses while remaining engaged in broader partnerships across industries and regions. We bring decades of experience in technology leadership, software development, and startup innovation to every engagement—right-sized, practical, and focused on outcomes that build trust and deliver measurable value.

### About Jesse

Reezon Technology is Jesse FitzGibbon's passion project.

Jesse is a seasoned technology leader with more than 20 years of experience building both technology and the teams that bring it to life. His career spans roles from Chief Technology Officer and Co-Founder, to Director of Products and Services, to Software Developer... serving organizations across commercial, government, and startup environments. Known for his ability to navigate complexity and drive clarity from ambiguity, Jesse combines strategic vision with hands-on technical depth to deliver meaningful results.

Guided by a “serve first” philosophy, Jesse takes pride in helping teams and businesses reach their potential through practical technology solutions and strong collaboration. His expertise includes leading enterprise digital transformation initiatives with a focus on cloud, AI, and the thoughtful application of emerging technologies to create real business value.

You can read more about Jesse and his experience at [jessefitz.me](https://jessefitz.me).
